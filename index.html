The Phulkari Edit

If the unstitched suit was the canvas, Phulkari was its most breathtaking masterpiece. It is the purest distillation of Punjab’s cultural heritage, and our master artisans are putting the final stitches on a limited-edition collection just for you.

Authentic Technique

Embroidered from the reverse side on hand-spun khaddar, using untwisted floss silk. You will soon be able to purchase suits featuring these exacting, centuries-old methods.
